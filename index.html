About Us

Established in 2022, Located Georgia's Samegrelo region, our headquarters proudly reside in the quaint village of Lesichene, Chkhorotsku. Our company boasts ownership of 45 hectares of prime blueberry orchards, cultivating a total of 120,000 plants comprising a diverse array of early varieties including Colibri, Sweetheart, New Hanover, Alix Blue, and Blue One. Our esteemed team comprises seasoned managers and skilled agricultural specialists dedicated to nurturing our blueberry plants with meticulous care. Each plant receives optimal conditions essential for yielding premium-quality blueberries.
